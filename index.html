About Parrot

Ever saw someone and wondered if they were into you too? Curious what's happening on campus?

Yeah, duh...dumb question, we know.
Parrot allows you to "Chirp" your crushes anonymously and Discover moves nearby.
If your crush chirps you back, it's a match!

Features

Discover & Endorse Moves

When you login into Parrot, the first screen you'll see is the local feed. Here you'll find moves happening on your campus. All moves are posted anonymously and live within a 2.5 radius of you. Moves can have a photo or location attached as well. Also, you can see specific activity from people in your Flock.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from Tinder?

Instead of swiping on cards within a mile radius, shoot your shot on people you see, already know of, or meet in real life. It's a social network where you can send anonymous match requests to people you actualy want to match with and stay in the loop with moves on campus.

How can I join Parrot?

Email us! We're looking for all kinds of skillsets, including but not limited to: marketing, media, software development and strategy

Why can I only use college email?

Signing up with a university email is a short term solution for ensuring safety of users and testing on college campuses.

Isn't an anonymous feed dangerous (i.e Yik Yak)?

While anonymous feeds in the past have had issues with cyberbullying and offensive content, Flock Activity acts as a way of identifying posts and holding user accountability.

Ok, but how is Parrot better than Tinder/Bumble?

With several ways to chirp users, Parrot aspires to bring true ownership of the match request–allowing you to validate interest in people you're more likely going to connect with on campus. Tinder is an app where people swipe out of boredom or for a confidence boost, instead Parrot provides a solution to being quickly judged by online profiles.

What's the difference between the ambassadors and the team?

A campus ambassador helps spread the presence of Parrot on campus and is a part of the overall team. While not everyone on the team is an ambassador :)

How does Parrot make money?

Subscription plan for premium features coming soon!

Who is Parrot for?

Parrot is an alternative to dating apps – it's for the person who may be curious in validating interest from their peers in real life without having to make a dating profile or be vocal. Parrot is also for the college student who wants to know what's going on around them on campus. In short, Parrot is for all and Parrot is for you!
